# Invariant-Core - Closure Invariants

---

## 1. Existence & Scope

* **CLOSURE_IS_ABSENCE_OF_INVALID_SURVIVAL_PATHS**
  Closure exists only when invalidity has no path to persist, propagate, leak, or return.

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_AN_ENFORCED_CONSTRAINT_FIELD**
  Closure requires active constraints that determine whether states are admissible.

* **CLOSURE_IS_NOT_MERE_ASSERTED_COMPLETION**
  Closure cannot be declared by preference, appearance, or local confidence without enforcement.

---

## 2. Constraint Enforcement

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_COMPLETE_CONSTRAINT_ENFORCEMENT**
  Closure requires every relevant constraint to be enforced against the states it governs.

* **CLOSURE_FAILS_WHEN_CONSTRAINTS_ARE_OPTIONAL**
  Closure cannot hold where required constraints can be ignored, suspended, or bypassed.

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_VALIDITY_AT_EVALUATION_BOUNDARIES**
  Closure depends on valid states being distinguished from invalid states at enforced boundaries.

---

## 3. Boundary Sealing

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_SEALED_BOUNDARIES**
  Every boundary relevant to the system must prevent invalid passage.

* **CLOSURE_FAILS_AT_WEAK_SEALS**
  A weak seal breaks closure when it allows invalid or unresolved states to pass forward.

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_NO_BYPASS_PATHS**
  No path may allow a state to avoid the constraints that determine its admissibility.

---

## 4. Invalidity Elimination

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_INVALIDITY_ELIMINATION**
  Invalid states must be eliminated, blocked, or collapsed into invariant-preserving form.

* **CLOSURE_CANNOT_CONTAIN_INVALID_STATES**
  A system is not closed while invalid states can remain inside it.

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_NO_LATENT_INVALIDITY**
  Apparent consistency is not closure if hidden invalidity can still survive.

---

## 5. Pressure Resolution

* **CLOSURE_INCREASES_THROUGH_PRESSURE_RESOLUTION**
  Closure increases when pressure resolves into validity or is eliminated as invalid.

* **CLOSURE_IS_BLOCKED_BY_UNRESOLVED_PRESSURE**
  Closure cannot complete while unresolved pressure remains hidden, deferred, or unvalidated.

* **CLOSURE_FAILS_WHEN_PRESSURE_LEAKS**
  Leaked pressure prevents closure because unresolved burden survives in relocated form.

---

## 6. Recursion & Re-Entry

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_NO_INVALID_REENTRY**
  Invalid states must not re-enter through recursive paths after rejection.

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_RECURSIVE_SEALING**
  Recursive processes must enforce constraints on every pass where invalidity could return.

* **CLOSURE_FAILS_UNDER_RECURSIVE_DRIFT**
  Closure cannot hold when recursion accumulates inconsistency or preserves unresolved burden.

---

## 7. Layer Integrity

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_FULL_STACK_VALIDITY**
  Closure must hold across all applicable layers of the invariant stack.

* **CLOSURE_CANNOT_OVERRIDE_LOWER_LAYER_INVALIDITY**
  Higher-layer acceptance cannot close a system that violates lower-layer constraints.

* **CLOSURE_STRENGTH_INCREASES_WITH_CONSTRAINT_DEPTH**
  Closure becomes stricter as constraints become less negotiable in deeper layers.

---

## 8. Truth Formation

* **CLOSURE_DEFINES_THE_BOUNDARY_OF_TRUTH**
  Closure defines the enforced boundary within which surviving states can be true.

* **CLOSURE_PRECEDES_FULL_TRUTH_SELECTION**
  Truth cannot fully close until invalid survival paths have been removed.

* **CLOSURE_LEAVES_ONLY_INVARIANT_PRESERVING_STATES**
  Complete closure leaves only states that preserve all applicable invariants.

---

## 9. Stability & Consistency

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_GLOBAL_CONSISTENCY**
  Closure requires surviving states to remain mutually consistent across the system.

* **CLOSURE_REJECTS_FALSE_CLOSURE**
  Apparent consistency must not be treated as closure when invalidity remains unresolved.

* **CLOSURE_STABILIZES_BY_REMOVING_INVALID_SURVIVAL**
  Closure supports stability by eliminating the paths through which invalidity can persist.

---

## 10. Operational Validity

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_EXPLICIT_EXIT_BEHAVIOR**
  Exits must resolve, eliminate, or contain pressure without allowing invalid leakage.

* **CLOSURE_REQUIRES_NO_UNFILTERED_PROPAGATION**
  No state may propagate without passing all relevant validity checks.

* **CLOSURE_IS_COMPLETE_ONLY_WHEN_INVALIDITY_CANNOT_RETURN**
  Closure is complete only when invalidity has no remaining path to persist, propagate, leak, bypass, or re-enter.

---

# Final Compression

> Closure is the absence of invalid survival paths.
> It requires enforced constraints,
> sealed boundaries,
> resolved pressure,
> eliminated invalidity,
> and no bypass, leakage, latent contradiction, or recursive re-entry.
