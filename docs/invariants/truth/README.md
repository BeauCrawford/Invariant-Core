# Invariant-Core - Truth Invariants

---

## 1. Survival & Enforcement

* **TRUTH_SURVIVES_COMPLETE_ENFORCEMENT**
  A state is true only if it survives complete constraint enforcement across all applicable layers.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_INVARIANT_PRESERVATION**
  Truth requires preservation of every active invariant that applies to the state.

* **TRUTH_IS_NOT_ASSERTED_OR_CHOSEN**
  Truth cannot be created by assertion, preference, authority, or interpretation alone.

---

## 2. Global Consistency

* **TRUTH_IS_GLOBALLY_CONSISTENT**
  A true state must remain consistent across the entire enforced structure.

* **TRUTH_CANNOT_BE_FRAGMENTED**
  A state cannot be true in one enforced region while violating constraints in another.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_CROSS_BOUNDARY_ALIGNMENT**
  Truth must remain admissible across all boundaries it touches or crosses.

---

## 3. Invalidity Elimination

* **TRUTH_EXCLUDES_INVALIDITY**
  No invalid state can belong to truth.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_INVALID_POSSIBILITY_ELIMINATION**
  Truth is defined only after invalid possibilities have been eliminated, blocked, or collapsed.

* **TRUTH_CANNOT_CONTAIN_LATENT_INVALIDITY**
  A state is not fully true while hidden invalidity can persist beneath apparent consistency.

---

## 4. Pressure Resolution

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_PRESSURE_RESOLUTION**
  Truth requires unresolved pressure to resolve into invariant-preserving form or be eliminated.

* **TRUTH_CANNOT_FORM_THROUGH_LEAKAGE**
  Leaked pressure does not produce truth because unresolved burden survives in relocated form.

* **TRUTH_ONLY_INCLUDES_RESOLVED_POSSIBILITY**
  A possibility participates in truth only after it has resolved without violating constraints.

---

## 5. Closure & Survival Paths

* **TRUTH_EXISTS_INSIDE_CLOSURE**
  Truth is the set of states that remain inside closure after invalid survival paths are removed.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_NO_INVALID_REENTRY**
  Truth cannot hold while invalidity can re-enter after rejection.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_NO_BYPASS_PATHS**
  Truth cannot hold while any path allows constraint enforcement to be bypassed.

---

## 6. Layer Integrity

* **TRUTH_RESPECTS_LAYER_ORDER**
  Truth must satisfy the invariant stack from higher-layer governance through lower-layer necessity.

* **TRUTH_CANNOT_OVERRIDE_LOWER_LAYER_CONSTRAINTS**
  Higher-layer acceptance cannot make a lower-layer violation true.

* **TRUTH_RIGIDITY_INCREASES_WITH_CONSTRAINT_DEPTH**
  Truth becomes less negotiable as constraint and authority converge in deeper layers.

---

## 7. Transformation & Recursion

* **TRUTH_IS_PRESERVED_UNDER_VALID_TRANSFORMATION**
  A true state remains true only under transformations that preserve all applicable invariants.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_RECURSIVE_STABILITY**
  Recursive processes must preserve truth and cannot reintroduce invalidity.

* **TRUTH_CANNOT_DEPEND_ON_INVALID_PATHS**
  No truth claim may depend on a transition path that violates constraints.

---

## 8. Semantics & Interpretation

* **TRUTH_IS_NOT_LOCAL_INTERPRETATION**
  Truth cannot be reduced to an unresolved or locally convenient interpretation.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_SEMANTIC_CONSISTENCY**
  Truth requires meanings to remain stable under all relevant valid interpretations.

* **TRUTH_EXCLUDES_SEMANTIC_CONTRADICTION**
  A state cannot be true if its meaning entails contradiction under enforced semantics.

---

## 9. Operational Validity

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_ADMISSIBILITY**
  A true state must be admissible under all active constraints that govern it.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_BOUNDARY_VALIDATION**
  A state cannot be treated as true until relevant boundary crossings have been validated.

* **TRUTH_REQUIRES_NO_UNRESOLVED_CONTRADICTION**
  Truth cannot contain contradiction that has not been eliminated, resolved, or proven impossible.

---

## 10. Finality & Compression

* **TRUTH_IS_SURVIVING_STRUCTURE**
  Truth is the surviving structure left after enforcement removes invalid possibility.

* **TRUTH_IS_DEFINED_BY_NON_ELIMINATION**
  Truth is defined by what cannot be eliminated by complete constraint enforcement.

* **TRUTH_CLOSES_WHEN_INVALIDITY_HAS_NO_SURVIVAL_PATH**
  Truth closes only when invalidity has nowhere left to persist, propagate, leak, or return.

---

# Final Compression

> Truth is invariant-preserving survival after complete constraint enforcement.
> It is not asserted or chosen.
> It remains only when pressure resolves,
> invalidity is eliminated,
> closure is complete,
> and no contradiction, bypass, leakage, or invalid re-entry remains.
